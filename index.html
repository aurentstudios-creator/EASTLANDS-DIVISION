Welcome to Eastlands Division

Transforming lives, sharing hope, and serving our communities in Eastlands with love and excellence.

"Heart to God, Hand to Man" — Our division serves over 15 corps and centers across Eastlands region.

🌟 Our Mission

To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and meet human needs in His name without discrimination.

📍 Coverage

Eastlands, including Buruburu, Kayole, Umoja, Donholm, and surrounding communities.

🙏 Weekly Services

Sunday worship, Wednesday prayer meetings, and community outreach programs.
