Transforming lives and communities across Eastlands through faith, hope, and practical service.

Our Mission

To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and meet human needs in His name without discrimination across Eastlands region.

Our Reach

Serving 15+ corps and centers across Buruburu, Kayole, Umoja, Donholm, Jericho, Embakasi, and beyond.

Our Impact

Over 5,000 lives touched annually through food programs, education support, and spiritual guidance.
